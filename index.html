About the Program

AI House is a 3–6 month immersive residency for aspiring AI engineers and recent graduates. Live in a collaborative co-living space, work on real AI projects, and get access to cloud infrastructure, GitHub, and expert mentorship – all while exploring innovative fields like agriculture, retail, and robotics.

Residency Highlights

Affordable Living

Residency fee is $2,000 per month, including light snacks and morning breakfast. Lunch and dinner can be catered for an additional charge.

Real-World Projects

Build AI MVPs for retail, grocery automation, LLMs, and auto safety with mentorship from top industry leaders.

Cloud & Tools Access

Get free access to AWS/GCP, GitHub repositories, and GPU resources for training and deploying AI models.

New: AgTech Field Experience

Join field trips to Brentwood's peach farms and learn how AI-powered water management and smart irrigation systems are revolutionizing agriculture. Use real farm data to build your own precision agriculture solutions.

Key Projects

Agentic AI

SituAware Auto

AI-based situational awareness for vehicle safety and smarter autonomous driving decisions.

AI Bootcamp Builder

Design structured bootcamp content and teach real-world AI skills to future cohorts.

Join the Cohort
